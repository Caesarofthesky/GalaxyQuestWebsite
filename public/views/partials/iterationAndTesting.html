  • Iteration Based Implementation

    • There are two theories behind implementing a machine learning algorithm. The first is through careful planning before implementation. The advantages of this strategy include likely producing a more scalable and elegant algorithm. The dangers of this approach misprioritization, given the task ahead is relatively hazy. Our recommended strategy is a 'build-and'fux" strategy. In this implementation, the goal is to quickly implementa and algorithm, and use extensive testing to determine growth areas. The advantage of this approach is most time is spent on relevant tasks, and it allows for a greater understanding of timelines.
